About Malladhi Motors

Over a decade of trusted automotive care in the heart of Autonagar, Guntur.

Our Story

Guntur's Trusted
Auto Experts

Malladhi Motors Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a single mission: to give every car owner in Guntur access to authorised-quality service at fair prices. Based in Autonagar — the automotive hub of Guntur — we have grown from a small workshop to a full-service automotive centre trusted by hundreds of customers.

We believe that great car care shouldn't mean inflated bills or voided warranties. That's why we use only genuine OEM parts, follow manufacturer service schedules, and maintain complete transparency at every step.

Whether it's a minor scratch or a major engine rebuild — we treat every vehicle with the same level of care and expertise.
